Our Story

Zilisimenye, Non-Profit Organization

Zilisimenye Elderly Home was established to accomodate senior citizens and look after the neglected and deserted elderly in the communities. We approach each resident as an individual, to respect their independence, privacy and freedom of choice in meeeting their physical/mental, cultural and spiritual needs.

Our Mission

We strive to accomodate and take care of the neglected and deserted elderly in the communities.

Volunteer

Volunteering is not just about giving your time; it's about making a difference in someone's life. It's about lending a helping hand to those in need and bringing joy to their hearts. Whether it's spending time with elderly residents, organizing community events, or simply lending an ear to listen, every act of volunteering contributes to building a stronger, more compassionate community.

When you volunteer, you're not only enriching the lives of others but also enhancing your own. It's an opportunity to learn new skills, expand your network, and gain a deeper understanding of the world around you. Volunteering can be a transformative experience that leaves a lasting impact on both the giver and the receiver.

No matter how big or small your contribution may seem, every act of kindness matters. Together, we can create a brighter future for our community and make the world a better place for generations to come.
